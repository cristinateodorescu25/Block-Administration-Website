Latest news

Gas supply suspended

Due to a pipe leakage in the surrounding area of our apartments block, there will be undergoing reparations, which will result in suspending the gas supply during the 30th of March, hours 10am to 5pm. We apologize for the inconvenience.

Spring planting

Following the arrival of spring, the administration of the block decided to improve the aspect of the exterior ground in the proximity, by planting new and diverse plant seeds. Reconstruction of the interior environment will also take place, as most last year's plants withered due to a flea infection.

Please contribute to the budget for this operation with 5 dollars.

Spring exterminator raids

Due to the previously mentioned flora infestation and thus destruction of the inner perimeter plants, exterminators had been called to run a cleaning procedure on the remaining grounds.

Please contribute to the budget for this operation with 10 dollars.

Recycling containers

Considering the amount of unrecycled garbage, the block administration decided to form a partnership with a recycling garbage collector non-governamental organization to place universal plastic, paper and glass containers on the ground floor, to keep the rubbish sorted. Please feel free to use the containers, noting the volume.

The flat amounting to the most recycled materials wins a prize!
